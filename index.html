Renatech-Dev is a team of web developers within the Renatech network, dedicated to improving the working conditions of researchers in various labs across the network. The team works on a variety of projects aimed at streamlining research processes and enhancing collaboration among scientists.
REPOTECH

On the user interface, any registered user can send requests for projects, feasibilities, manage their pending requests and projects, contact administrator for anything, and much more.

On the administrator side, all admins can access to informations, depending on their role, including project management, pending requests, users list...

Every information can be extracted from website to a tab file format like XLSX or CSV.

This application is used by all laboratories within the Renatech Network.

The app is also able to generate stats and graphics about various data related to its database.


In 2023, the REPOTECH app counts :

  • Users : 3 500+
  • Projects submitted in 2022 : 300
  • Pending projects in 2022 : 920
  • Projects submitted since the first deployment : 2 300+
  • Projects with foreign partners : 680
CROSS

On the clean room side, any registered user can access to tools they have right to use. The system will record the products and duration of the experiment.

On the administrator side, all admins can access to information, depending on their role, including logs, experiments duration, prices, users...

Every information can be extracted from website to a tab file format like XLSX or CSV.

In 2023, the CROSS app will manage :

  • Boxes 14
  • Tools 50
  • Operation by year 30 000


Currently, this project is in production in the IEMN lab in Villeneuve d'Ascq.

OUR PROJECTS

REPOTECH
Renatech network project management tool

The REPOTECH application gives the ability to users to manage their projects, make requests depending on what they need... it also allows administrators of the Renatech network's facilities to manage project requests, feasibility studies, user profiles, network statistics, and more. Its user-friendly interface and powerful features make it an indispensable tool for efficiently managing the network's projects and resources.

More info Video
CROSS
Clean ROom Service Support


CROSS is a solution to log operation inside the cleanroom. It's composed of two parts, a box to collect the user's need in the clean room (tools, project, ...) and a website to consult the operations performed. Boxes in use are presented in the video or their workflow in the poster.

More info Video Poster
Publis-HAL
Display HAL references


Publis-HAL is a website to display the references of a laboratory from HAL. It allows to make some research inside all the references or to display one of the most pre-build group of reference like all the reference of a research team.
